FlightLogger lets you record tracklogs with your phone. 

Get Started in Four Easy Steps

Add Gliders

Add your gliders/wings so you can link them to flightlogs.

Record

You can lock the screen to save power and use other apps while recording.

Edit

Trim/split tracklog and add what you like.

Export

Export unsigned .kml files to iCloud Drive or directly to flightlig.org

Made for Recreational Aerial Sports

Includes PG, HG, SPG, Sailplane and Baloon categories, all with dual/tandem options.

Powerfull Editing Features

After competing a flight, you can add the information you want to accompany the tracklog.

crop

Edit Tracklog

Trim and split into multiple flights. Lets you know the exact stats for each trip.

Select your Glider

Track which glider/wing you used for each flight.

Add Photos

Add a photo, description and other informationo to each log.

Describe Flight

Make a note of what you did and what you can imporve.

Exports

Exports can be automoated after each flight, or done manually.

iCloud Drive

Export .kml files to your local file-system or iCloud Drive, where they sync automatically to your computer.

web

flightlog.org

Export tracklogs, including images and all directly to flightlog.org at the click of a button.

Note that .kml files are not signed and cannot be used in competitions or open distance leagues.
