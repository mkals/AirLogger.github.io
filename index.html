FlightLogger

This app was made to make the logging experience as effortless as possible for aerial sports. Start recording your flight at the tap of a button. When you are done, press "done" and add the information you want, such as a description, an image, or which glider/wing you were using. If you have done more than one flight in one go, you can easily split the tracklog into multiple flights.

All your flights are stored on your device and synced with iCloud. You can also export your flights as a standard .kml file that can be read by programs such as Google Earth. You can also upload the log (including .kml, image, description and vehicle) directly to flightlog.org.

Warning: the .kml files the app generates are unsigned. This means that they cannot be used in any competitive setting such as XC competitions, open distance leagues etc.
